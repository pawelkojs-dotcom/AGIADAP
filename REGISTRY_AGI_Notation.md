# REGISTRY_AGI_Notation — Symbols & Glossary

## Symbols
| Symbol | Meaning | Notes |
|--------|---------|-------|
| σ      | coherence / belief state | normalized; vector/graph |
| Θ      | information temperature   | exploration amplitude |
| γ      | medium viscosity          | damping / temporal metric |
| F      | free energy               | E - ΘS |
| E      | energy (task/consistency) | domain-specific |
| S      | configurational entropy   | belief entropy |

## Terms
- **Ecotone:** region where both ||∇σ|| and ||∇Θ|| exceed thresholds; productive boundary.
- **Glass transition:** metastable plateau at low Θ and high γ.
- **Anti-scaling (AGI):** γ_crit(N) ∝ 1/N; larger N needs smaller γ for coherence.
